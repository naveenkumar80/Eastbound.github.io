Discovery Trips

Our endeavor to position Eastbound as a market and thought leader in the space of Experiential & Discovery Travel. Our unique Journey's are created for the discerning traveler by a highly passionate team, who believes in the ethos of inspiration and innovation. These hub of ideas converges into some of the most enthralling travel experiences. Outlined below is a glimpse of our offerings...

DESIGN, ARCHITECHTURE &

PHOTOGRAPHY

Art Deco to Victorian, Mughal to miniature; the sub-continent’s rich heritage and vivid design and architecture will leave you stunned. Uncover the real soul of the country through its design, architecture and photography tours.



SACRED AND SPIRITUAL JOURNEYS

Find out what inner peace and spirituality means from the country that invented those words! Rich in traditions, rituals and holy places, let us find the sacred destinations in India and the sub-continent for you that suit your frame of mind and to maximize your spiritual experience. Find your inner peace, go on a deep inner spiritual journey and re-discover yourself.

THE GREAT INDIAN FESTIVALS CIRCUIT

India simply bursts with festivals – each month, each day there is a festival or an occasion that is celebrated in India. From the colourful Holi, to the sparkling fireworks of Diwali, the many religions of India celebrate a number of festivals with great pride and oneness.



CONNECTING PEOPLE

Experiential travel gets a whole new meaning as you visit monuments and meet people in their local setting and seeing the way they live, work and play! We will connect you with the people who matter, from various walks of life who will impact you, engage you and will remain your friends and stay with you for a long time to come

CULINARY & FOOD VOYAGES

Culinary & Food Voyages highlight the amazing food heritage and experiences of India and the sub-continent. We will showcase the local produce and amazing variety of spices and herbs, take you to sparkling food soirees in India where food curators will tell you knowledgably about food; and you will eat as the locals do.

WELLNESS & RENEWAL VACATIONS

Wellness and Renewal Vacations will renew your mind, body and soul with carefully chosen destinations, selected for their deep sense of serenity and a palpable spiritual energy.
