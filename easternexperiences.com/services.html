Explorations

Established by a group of highly respected travel professionals, EE is defined by its passion and commitment to create unparalleled journeys of discovery…of people, cultures, history, food, wellness and the spiritual, that translate into timeless stories.
Eastern Explorations lives through its deep-rooted network with ‘local owners of the heritage’ to deliver on this promise.

Cultural and Spiritual immersion

East is a captivating realm, deeply steeped in cultural and spiritual richness, inviting travelers to embark on a profound journey of immersion. Its diverse tapestry of traditions, rituals, and beliefs beckons the curious souls to embrace the essence of humanity. From the ancient temples of India resonating with chants and incense to the serene Zen gardens of Japan, each corner of the East offers an opportunity for introspection and soulful discovery.


Nature and wildlife

Eastern landscapes teem with awe-inspiring nature and abundant wildlife, creating a haven for nature enthusiasts and wildlife aficionados alike. From the breathtaking peaks of the Himalayas to the lush rainforests of Southeast Asia, the East presents a captivating tableau of biodiversity. Be enchanted by the majestic elephants roaming freely, the elusive big cats prowling through dense jungles, and the colorful array of tropical birds filling the skies.


Culinary explorations

From the delectable street food stalls of bustling markets to the opulent dining rooms of grand palaces, Eastern cuisine offers an exquisite array of gastronomic delights. Savor the bold and aromatic curries of India, the delicate sushi and sashimi of Japan, the savory noodle dishes of China, and the delectable street eats of Southeast Asia.

Photography tours

Our photography tour offers unparalleled access to mesmerizing landscapes, vibrant cultures, and captivating moments, providing the perfect canvas for you to hone your skills and unleash your artistic vision. Led by expert photographers, you'll receive personalized guidance and tips to master composition, lighting, and storytelling, ensuring your photographs tell compelling stories of your travels

Educational Travel

Designed to inspire curiosity and foster a love for knowledge, this journey combines the joy of exploration with enriching educational experiences. Led by experienced educators and experts, the tour takes students on a captivating adventure, where they'll engage with historical landmarks, scientific wonders, cultural heritage, and local communities.
